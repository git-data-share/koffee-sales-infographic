\# Koffee Sales – 2023 Half Time Review (Power BI Infographic)



!\[Power BI Report](https://github.com/git-data-share/koffee-sales-infographic/raw/main/images/infographic-full.png)





\## Overview

This project presents a data-driven sales analysis of Koffee’s New Florida Sales Region for the first half of 2023.  

It highlights sales performance, product insights, customer behavior, and future forecasts to support better business decisions.





\## Problem Statement

Koffee’s Florida expansion lacks a centralized view to track performance across stores, products, and time.



\- Sales vary across locations and time periods  

\- Product performance is inconsistent  

\- Revenue drivers are not clearly identified  

\- Forecasting future growth is difficult  



A unified dashboard is required to generate actionable insights.





\## Objective

\- Track key metrics like total sales, purchases, and order size  

\- Identify top-performing products and categories  

\- Analyze store-level performance (Miami, Orlando, Tampa)  

\- Understand customer behavior by time of day  

\- Forecast full-year sales performance  

\- Provide data-driven recommendations  





\## Key Questions

\- How much revenue and how many purchases have been generated?  

\- What is the average order size?  

\- Which products and categories drive revenue?  

\- What time of day generates the most sales?  

\- Which days perform best and worst?  

\- How do stores compare in performance?  

\- What is the expected full-year revenue?  





\## Key Metrics

\- Total Purchases: 149,116  

\- Total Sales: $698,812  

\- Average Order Size: $4.69  

\- New Stores: 3  

\- Menu Items: 80

\## Key Insights



\*\*Sales Performance\*\*

\- Strong early growth with $698K+ revenue  

\- Sales improved significantly in April–June  

\- Business depends on high transaction volume  



\*\*Product Insights\*\*

\- Coffee is the top revenue category  

\- Tea is second highest  

\- Few products contribute majority of revenue  

\- Performance varies by location  



\*\*Store Insights\*\*

\- Orlando has highest order value  

\- Miami has lowest → improvement opportunity  



\*\*Time Insights\*\*

\- Peak sales at 10 AM  

\- Active window: 7 AM – 8 PM  

\- Only 3.6% sales outside this window  



\*\*Day Insights\*\*

\- Highest: Monday  

\- Lowest: Saturday  



\*\*Forecast\*\*

\- Expected: $1.4M  

\- Stretch goal: $1.59M  

\- Tampa projected highest  





\## Challenges

\- Low average order value  

\- Dependency on top products  

\- Underperforming time slots  

\- Variation across locations  

\- Weak contribution from some categories  





\## Recommendations



\*\*Sales Strategy\*\*

\- Increase basket size using combos  

\- Promote high-margin items  



\*\*Product Strategy\*\*

\- Focus on Coffee \& Tea  

\- Improve or remove low-performing categories  

\- Create location-based product strategies  



\*\*Operations Strategy\*\*

\- Adjust store hours  

\- Focus staffing during peak time  

\- Reduce low-value operating hours  



\*\*Store Strategy\*\*

\- Replicate Orlando success  

\- Improve Miami performance  



\*\*Growth Strategy\*\*

\- Reforecast regularly  

\- Leverage strong Q2 momentum
